Benefits of Athena
==================

**Benefits of Athena**

**Enhances User Experience:**
		
	Conversational interaction enhances user experience beyond swipe and click.

**Reduce data complexity:**

	Data from customer,competition,industry updates and subscribed sources through one integrated platform will provides 360 degree view of the business.

**Increased BI Adoption:**

	Delivering instant actionable insights will increase the adoption and drive information-driven decision making.

**Empowerd Users:**

	Empower the users with true-self-service Insights everywhere to make informed decisions always.
