Benefits of Libro
=================

**User Experience**

	Conversational interaction enhances user experience beyond swipe and click.

**Library on the Go**

	Kids can search and reserve books easily,adults can access the local library more frequently even while driving.

**Increase in usage**

	Ease of access and moblity will increase the readers and library access

**Helps in Diversity**

	Non-english speaking/reading users can access the library through voice and access books(Ex: Spanish).

**Ensuring the Future**

	Natural language and conversational is the future of user interaction,ensures the future for the libraries.
