Factro
======

 **Voice based solution to monitor, measure and manage the business process in the manufacturing plant**


**Process Insights**

	*Factro helps to manage the shop floor operations effieciently from anywherewith a simple voice conversation*

	* Intergrated end-to-end process
	* Core process digitization
	* Collaboration with ecosystem
	* Visibility across value chain

**Over view of Factro**

	* Supplier - *Spend & Procurement Analytics*

	* Manufacturing - *Plant Analytics*

	* Warehouse - *Supply Chain Analytics*

	* Dealer - *Logistics Analytics*

	* Customer - *Warranty Analytics*
